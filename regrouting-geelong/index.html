How much does shower regrouting cost? +

Pricing depends on shower size, the condition of the existing grout, and whether silicone and waterproofing top-ups are needed. Most standard residential shower regrouts in Geelong fall in a predictable range. We provide a fixed quote after a quick site visit so there are no surprises.

Why does shower grout fail? +

Daily water exposure breaks down the grout seal over time. Once water gets behind the tiles, mould grows, the bond weakens, and tiles loosen. Poor original installation, building movement, and harsh cleaners all speed it up. Regrouting strips the failed material and restores the seal properly.

Do I need to replace my tiles too? +

Almost never. Regrouting renews the lines between your tiles and the silicone seals at corners and floor joints. Your existing tiles stay in place. We only replace tiles if individual ones are cracked or loose, and we work with you on matching.

Can you fix mould between the tiles? +

Yes. Mould lives inside the porous old grout, so cleaning it just hides the problem. We strip the affected grout out, treat the underlying surface, and replace it with mould-resistant Mapei grout. The result lasts because the source is gone.
