## How to use these numbers

- **Be specific.** "One to two days" beats "fast". "Within 24 hours" beats "quick response".
- **Name the brand.** "Mapei grout" beats "premium grout" every time.
- **Quote the service area in full at least once per page.** Geelong as anchor; Melbourne, Ballarat, surrounding regions as the perimeter.
- **Don't combine review counts across platforms.** Google reviews are Google reviews. Facebook reviews are Facebook reviews. Schema and body copy treat them separately or not at all.
- **If you need a number you don't have, leave it out.** Vague is better than wrong - and wrong is what just got us into trouble (see CRM/clients/Grout Academy/notes.md, 2026-04-30 entry).
