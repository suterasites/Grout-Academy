Why Geelong Grout Fails

Two stories cover most of the work we do in Geelong. The first is the older homes in Newtown, East Geelong, Belmont and Geelong West. Tiles on cement render. Sometimes no waterproofing layer underneath at all. Water gets in, the grout fails, the mould moves into the substrate.

The second is the newer estates - Armstrong Creek, Charlemont, Mount Duneed, Grovedale. The build quality on the tiles is fine. The grout and silicone the volume builders use is not. It breaks down inside five to seven years and the bathroom starts looking tired before the rest of the house does.

Either way the fix is the same. Strip the old grout and silicone out properly. Rebuild with Mapei. Do it right so it lasts a decade, not eighteen months.

FAQs - Geelong

Common Questions

Do you cover all of Geelong?

Yes. Geelong is our primary service area. That covers Newtown, Belmont, East Geelong, Highton, Geelong West, Manifold Heights, Herne Hill, Hamlyn Heights, Whittington, St Albans Park, Armstrong Creek, Charlemont, Mount Duneed, Grovedale and the rest of the city and suburbs.

How quickly can you get to a job in Geelong?

Quotes within 24 hours. Standard booking lead time is one to two weeks because we book jobs back to back to keep the workmanship right. Urgent leaks, call us, we will tell you what we can do.

Why does Geelong grout fail so often?

Two main reasons. Older Geelong homes, Newtown, East Geelong, Belmont, often have tiles on cement render with no modern waterproofing layer. Newer estate builds use cheap builder grade grout and silicone that breaks down inside five to seven years. We strip it back and rebuild with Mapei.

Can you regrout a shower without removing the tiles?

Yes. Regrouting is exactly that. We strip out the old grout and silicone, leave the tiles in place, and put fresh Mapei grout and silicone back in. We only pull tiles if a tile is loose or damaged underneath.

Do you handle commercial work in Geelong?

Yes. Apartments, motels, gyms, body corporates, hospitality bathrooms. We work around opening hours where we need to. Same product, same standard, just scheduled to fit the operation.
